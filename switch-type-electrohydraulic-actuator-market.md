Linear Actuators are designed to convert hydraulic power into linear motion for applications such as opening/closing valves or moving equipment in a straight line. On the other hand, Rotary Actuators convert hydraulic power into rotary motion to control the movement of valves or other components that require rotational motion. Both types of actuators play a crucial role in industrial automation and control systems.</p></p>
