<p><p>Automotive Active Roll Stabilization technology utilizes either Rotary Actuators or Linear Actuators to reduce body roll and improve vehicle stability during cornering. Rotary Actuators operate by twisting a shaft to counteract the forces acting on the vehicle, while Linear Actuators use linear motion to achieve the same goal. Both types of actuators work in tandem with sensors and onboard computer systems to adjust suspension settings in real-time, providing a smoother and safer driving experience. These technologies are increasingly adopted in high-performance and luxury vehicles.</p></p>
