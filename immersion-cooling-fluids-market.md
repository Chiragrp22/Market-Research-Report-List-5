<p><p>Immersion cooling fluids market is primarily dominated by two types of fluids - Hydrocarbon and Fluorocarbon. Hydrocarbon-based fluids, such as mineral oil, are biodegradable and have high thermal conductivity making them a popular choice for immersion cooling systems. On the other hand, Fluorocarbon-based fluids, like perfluorocarbons, offer better chemical stability and do not react with metals, making them suitable for high-performance cooling applications. Both types of fluids play a crucial role in the immersion cooling industry by efficiently dissipating heat from electronic components.</p></p>
