<p><ul><li>Acid Phytase</li><li>Alkaline Phytase</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Powder phytases in the market can be categorized into two main types: acid phytase and alkaline phytase. Acid phytases are enzymes that work optimally under acidic conditions, while alkaline phytases function best in alkaline environments. Both types of phytases play a crucial role in breaking down phytic acid in plant-based feed and improving the availability of phosphorus and other nutrients for animals. These enzymes are commonly used in animal feed production to enhance nutrient absorption and promote overall health and growth in livestock.</p></p>
