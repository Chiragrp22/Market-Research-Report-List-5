<p><p>Percutaneous Coronary Intervention (PCI) Balloon Catheter market offers different types of balloon catheters based on their compliance level: Semi-compliant, Non-compliant, and Compliant. Semi-compliant balloon catheters have a limited ability to expand, suitable for treating lesions in curved vessels. Non-compliant balloon catheters have a rigid structure that allows for maximum vessel dilatation and are ideal for calcified lesions. Compliant balloon catheters offer a balance between flexibility and expansion capability, making them versatile for various lesion types. Each type serves a specific purpose in PCI procedures.</p></p>
