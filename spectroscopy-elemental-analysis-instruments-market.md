Spectrometers are used for measuring the interaction of electromagnetic radiation with matter, while gas analyzers are used for detecting and analyzing gases. Thermal analyzers measure changes in physical or chemical properties with temperature. Conductivity and resistivity meters measure the ability of a substance to conduct electricity, while pH meters measure the acidity or alkalinity of a solution. Titrators are used to determine the concentration of a substance in a solution by reacting it with a standardized solution. Refractometers measure the refractive index of a substance, and other instruments in the market cater to specific analysis needs in various industries.</p></p>
