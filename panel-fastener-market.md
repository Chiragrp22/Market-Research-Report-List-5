<p><p>Panel fasteners refer to the various types of fasteners used for joining or securing panels or materials together. The types of panel fasteners include screws, nails, bolts, nuts and washers, anchors, rivets, and others. Screws are threaded fasteners used to fasten materials together, while nails are pointed metal fasteners driven into materials. Bolts are threaded fasteners with a head that requires a nut for tightening. Nuts and washers are used in conjunction with bolts for secure fastening. Anchors are fasteners used to secure objects to surfaces. Rivets are permanent fasteners that are inserted and compressed to hold materials together. Others refer to various types of specialized fasteners used for specific applications in panel assembly.</p></p>
