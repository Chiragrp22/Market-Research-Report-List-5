<p><strong>The Sapphire Spherical Lenses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Concave Lens</li><li>Convex Lens</li><li>Planoconvex Lens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sapphire spherical lenses come in various types catering to different optical needs. Concave lenses are thinner at the center and spread light out, commonly used in correcting nearsightedness. Convex lenses are thicker at the center and converge light, often used in correcting farsightedness. Planoconvex lenses have one flat surface and one convex surface, focusing parallel light rays to a single point. Each type of lens serves a specific purpose in various optical applications, providing solutions for different vision requirements.</p></p>
