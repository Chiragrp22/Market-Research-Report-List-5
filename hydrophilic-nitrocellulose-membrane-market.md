Membranes with pore sizes below 0.45 µm are ideal for applications requiring fine filtration such as protein and nucleic acid analysis. Those with pore sizes ranging from 0.45 to 6 µm are commonly used in particle retention and clarification processes. Membranes with pore sizes above 6 µm are typically used for large particle retention and prefiltration purposes in various industries such as pharmaceuticals and biotechnology.</p></p>
