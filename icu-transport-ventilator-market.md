<p><p>ICU Transport Ventilators are classified into three main types based on their operation mechanisms - Electronic, Pneumatic, and Electro-Pneumatic. Electronic ventilators operate using electronic controls and sensors to deliver precise ventilation settings. Pneumatic ventilators rely on compressed gas sources to drive the ventilation process. Electro-Pneumatic ventilators combine both electronic controls and pneumatic components for efficient and accurate ventilation delivery. Each type has its unique advantages and is chosen based on specific patient needs and requirements in the ICU transport setting.</p></p>
