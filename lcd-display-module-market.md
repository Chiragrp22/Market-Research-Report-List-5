<p><p>TN (Twisted Nematic) displays offer fast response times and low power consumption. STN (Super Twisted Nematic) displays provide improved brightness and viewing angles. VA (Vertical Alignment) displays have high contrast ratios and wide viewing angles. TFT (Thin Film Transistor) displays offer high resolutions and color accuracy. Other types of LCD display modules include IPS (In-Plane Switching) and OLED (Organic Light-Emitting Diode) displays, each with their own unique advantages in terms of performance and visual quality. These different types cater to various needs and preferences in the display market.</p></p>
