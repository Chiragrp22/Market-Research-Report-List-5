<p><p>Edible fiber market can be classified into two main types: soluble fiber and insoluble fiber. Soluble fiber dissolves in water and forms a gel-like substance in the intestines, helping to lower cholesterol levels and regulate blood sugar. Insoluble fiber, on the other hand, does not dissolve in water and helps to promote regular bowel movements and prevent constipation. Both types of fiber are essential for a healthy diet and provide various health benefits for the digestive system and overall well-being.</p></p>
