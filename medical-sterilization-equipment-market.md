<p><p>Medical sterilization equipment market includes various types such as steam sterilizers, ethylene oxide sterilizers, and others. Steam sterilizers use high-pressure steam to kill bacteria and other pathogens, making them effective for sterilizing medical equipment. Ethylene oxide sterilizers use a gas to kill microorganisms, making them suitable for heat-sensitive equipment. Other types of sterilization equipment may include dry heat sterilizers or ultraviolet sterilizers. Each type of sterilization equipment serves a specific purpose in ensuring the safety and efficacy of medical equipment and supplies.</p></p>
