<p><strong>The Electrochromic Window Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polymer Laminate</li><li>All-solid-state</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrochromic windows are of two main types: Polymer Laminate and All-solid-state. Polymer Laminate windows consist of a thin film of polymer sandwiched between two glass layers, allowing for a flexible and lightweight design. They are cost-effective and widely used in buildings. On the other hand, All-solid-state windows include inorganic materials with enhanced stability and durability. They are commonly used in high-tech applications where reliability is crucial. Both types of electrochromic windows offer energy efficiency and can control the amount of light and heat entering a space.</p></p>
