<p><p>Bottom-entry agitators are commonly used in industries for mixing and blending purposes. There are several types of bottom-entry agitators, including paddle agitators, turbine agitators, and magnetic agitators. Paddle agitators have blades that rotate around a shaft to mix materials, while turbine agitators use blades to create a vertical flow for blending. Magnetic agitators use magnetic fields to rotate a stir bar inside the vessel. Other types of bottom-entry agitators may include propeller agitators or helical agitators, each offering unique mixing capabilities for different applications.</p></p>
