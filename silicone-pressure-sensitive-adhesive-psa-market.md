<p><strong>The Silicone Pressure Sensitive Adhesive (PSA) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Addition-cure</li><li>Peroxide-cure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Silicone Pressure Sensitive Adhesive (PSA) is divided into two main types - Addition-cure and Peroxide-cure markets. Addition-cure PSAs are manufactured using platinum catalyzed hydrosilylation, providing excellent thermal and chemical resistance. These adhesives are commonly used in applications that require high temperature performance. On the other hand, Peroxide-cure PSAs are produced using a peroxide curing system, offering good adhesion and flexibility. They are often utilized in applications where low temperature resistance is critical. Both types cater to different market needs and requirements.</p></p>
