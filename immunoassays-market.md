Enzyme Immunoassay utilizes enzymes for detection, Fluorescence Immunoassay uses fluorescent labels, Chemiluminescence Immunoassay involves light emission reactions, Radioimmunoassay uses radioactive tracers, and Nephelometric Immunoassay measures light scattering to detect substances.
