<p><ul><li>Physical Sunscreen</li><li>Chemical Sunscreen</li><li>Physical and Chemical Sunscreen</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Physical sunscreen contains active mineral ingredients such as zinc oxide or titanium dioxide that sit on top of the skin and reflect UV rays. Chemical sunscreen contains organic compounds like oxybenzone or avobenzone that absorb UV rays and convert them into heat. Physical and chemical sunscreen combines both mineral and organic ingredients to provide broad-spectrum protection. Each type offers unique benefits and considerations, allowing consumers to choose the best option based on their preferences and skin sensitivities.</p></p>
