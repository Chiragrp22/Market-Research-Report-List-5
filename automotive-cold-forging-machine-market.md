<p><p>Automotive cold forging machines come in two main types: vertical and horizontal. Horizontal type machines, on the other hand, have a horizontal feeding mechanism, making them suitable for producing parts like automotive bolts and nuts. Both types of machines offer precise shaping and forming of metal parts through the cold forging process, catering to different requirements in the automotive industry.</p></p>
