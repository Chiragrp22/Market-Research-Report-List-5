<p><p>The wheelchair medical equipment market consists of two main types: electric and manual wheelchairs. Electric wheelchairs are powered by batteries and typically have a joystick for the user to control movement. They are suitable for individuals with limited upper body strength or mobility. Manual wheelchairs, on the other hand, are propelled by the user's own physical strength by pushing the wheels. They are more common and cost-effective than electric wheelchairs, making them a popular choice for many individuals with mobility challenges.</p></p>
