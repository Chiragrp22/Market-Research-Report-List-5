<p><p>PCIe controllers are essential components in modern computers that facilitate the communication between the system's CPU and storage devices. The three main market types for PCIe controllers are SCSI, SATA, and NVMe. SCSI controllers are widely used for connecting storage devices such as hard drives and tape drives. SATA controllers are commonly found in desktops and laptops for connecting hard drives and SSDs. NVMe controllers are rapidly gaining popularity due to their high speed and low latency, making them ideal for high-performance storage solutions.</p></p>
