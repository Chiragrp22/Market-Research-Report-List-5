<p><p>Needle-free drug injection systems market is segmented into jet-based, spring-based, laser-powered, and vibration-based injectors. Jet-based injectors use high pressure to deliver medication through the skin. Spring-based injectors use a spring mechanism to force medication into the skin. Laser-powered injectors utilize laser technology to create micropores in the skin for drug delivery. Vibration-based injectors use rapid vibrations to break the skin barrier. Each type offers a needle-free alternative to traditional injections, catering to different patient needs and preferences.</p></p>
