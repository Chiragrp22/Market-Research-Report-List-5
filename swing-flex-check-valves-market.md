<p><p>Swing Flex Check Valves come in three main types: Swing Single Disc Check Valves, Swing Double Disc Check Valves, and Swing Multi-disc Check Valves. These valves are designed to allow flow in one direction while preventing backflow. Swing Single Disc valves have a single disc that swings open for flow and closes to prevent backflow. Swing Double Disc valves have two discs for enhanced sealing. Swing Multi-disc valves have multiple discs for improved sealing and reliability, making them suitable for high-pressure applications.</p></p>
