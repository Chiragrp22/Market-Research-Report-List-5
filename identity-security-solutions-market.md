<p><p>Identity Governance and Administration (IGA) focuses on managing and controlling user access to ensure compliance with company policies. Identity and Access Management (IAM) focuses on securely managing user identities and their access to systems and applications. Privileged Access Management (PAM) involves managing and securing the privileged accounts and access of administrators. Active Directory Management and Security (ADMS) focuses on protecting and securing the Active Directory infrastructure to prevent unauthorized access and potential breaches. These solutions collectively aim to ensure comprehensive identity security within organizations.</p></p>
