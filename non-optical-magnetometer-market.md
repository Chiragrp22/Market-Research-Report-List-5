<p><strong>The Non-Optical Magnetometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absolute Magnetometer</li><li>Relative Magnetometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Non-Optical Magnetometer Market includes two types: Absolute Magnetometer and Relative Magnetometer. Absolute Magnetometer measures the absolute magnetic field strength without referencing an external magnetic field, making it suitable for standalone applications. On the other hand, Relative Magnetometer measures the relative change in magnetic field compared to an external reference, providing more accurate readings for applications requiring precise measurements. Both types cater to different market needs, with Absolute Magnetometer being used in independent settings and Relative Magnetometer in applications requiring a reference point.</p></p>
