<p><p>Yacht cranes can be categorized into four main types based on their mechanisms: hydraulic, manual, electric, and hybrid. Hydraulic yacht cranes utilize fluid power to lift heavy loads, providing efficient and powerful lifting capabilities. Manual yacht cranes require physical effort to operate and are often used for smaller vessels. Electric yacht cranes are powered by electricity and offer a balance between power and convenience. Hybrid yacht cranes combine different power sources to maximize efficiency and flexibility in lifting operations.</p></p>
