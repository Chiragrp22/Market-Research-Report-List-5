<p><p>High-performance air source heat pumps (ASHP) come in two main types: air-to-air and air-to-water. Air-to-air heat pumps heat and cool air directly for residential and commercial spaces. They are efficient and versatile, providing both heating and cooling solutions. On the other hand, air-to-water heat pumps transfer heat to a water-based system for space heating or domestic hot water. They are commonly used in buildings with underfloor heating or radiators. Both types offer energy-efficient solutions for reducing carbon emissions and lowering energy costs.</p></p>
