<p><p>Gas-welding hoses come in different grades, such as Grade R and Grade T. Grade R hoses are designed for use with acetylene gas, while Grade T hoses can be used with both acetylene and oxygen gases. Grade R hoses are recommended for repair and maintenance work, while Grade T hoses are suitable for production and fabricating applications. Both types of hoses are flexible, durable, and resistant to oils and grease, making them ideal for a variety of welding tasks.</p></p>
