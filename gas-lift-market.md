<p><ul><li>Continuous Flow</li><li>Intermittent Flow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gas lift systems are used in oil and gas production to increase the flow of fluids from reservoirs. There are two main types of gas lift markets: continuous flow and intermittent flow. Continuous flow systems involve a consistent injection of gas to lift liquids to the surface at a continuous rate. Intermittent flow systems involve periodic gas injections to lift liquids in intervals. Both markets cater to different production needs and well conditions, with continuous flow systems providing steady and consistent production while intermittent flow systems are more suitable for fluctuating flow rates.</p></p>
