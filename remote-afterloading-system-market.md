High Dose Rate (HDR) afterloaders deliver a high dose of radiation in a short amount of time, while Pulse Dose Rate (PDR) afterloaders deliver radiation in multiple smaller doses over a longer period. Both types of afterloaders offer benefits such as increased treatment accuracy, reduced radiation exposure to healthy tissues, and improved patient comfort. The market for HDR and PDR afterloaders continues to grow as advancements in technology enhance treatment outcomes.</p></p>
