<p><p>Harbour dredging can be classified into four main market types based on the purpose of the dredging activities. Capital dredging involves creating new harbours or deepening existing ones to accommodate larger vessels. Coastal protection dredging aims to protect coastlines from erosion by replenishing beaches with sand. Maintenance dredging involves removing sediment buildup to maintain navigational depth. Rivers & Lakes dredging focuses on maintaining waterways for transportation, irrigation, or wildlife habitat preservation. Each market type caters to specific needs and has its unique set of challenges and requirements.</p></p>
