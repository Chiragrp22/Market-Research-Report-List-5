<p><p>Zirconia crowns are a popular choice for dental restorations due to their durability and natural appearance. Two main types of zirconia crowns are layered zirconium crowns and monolithic zirconium crowns. Layered zirconium crowns are made up of a zirconia core covered with layers of porcelain, providing a more aesthetic look. Monolithic zirconium crowns, on the other hand, are made from a single block of zirconia, making them stronger and more resistant to chipping. Both types offer different benefits depending on the patient's needs and preferences.</p></p>
