<p><p>Foodborne pathogen test kits market includes types such as PCR and ELISA kits. ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay) kits detect proteins produced by pathogens, offering quick and reliable results. Both types of kits are widely used in food safety testing to identify harmful microorganisms like bacteria and viruses, ensuring the safety of food products in the market.</p></p>
