<p><p>The Pelvis External Fixation System market consists of two main types: Tubular and Circular. Tubular external fixation systems are made up of hollow tubes that are connected with pins and clamps to stabilize the pelvis. On the other hand, Circular external fixation systems use rings that are attached to the pelvis with wires or pins to provide stability. Both types of systems are commonly used in orthopedic surgeries to treat pelvic fractures and dislocations, with each offering different advantages and applications in clinical practice.</p></p>
