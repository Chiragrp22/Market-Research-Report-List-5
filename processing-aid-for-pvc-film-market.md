<p><strong>The Processing Aid for PVC Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Prill</li><li>Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Processing aid for PVC film comes in two main types: prill and powder. Prill processing aid is granular and can easily mix with PVC resin, providing excellent dispersibility. It is commonly used in extrusion processes. Powder processing aid, on the other hand, is finely ground and offers better flowability. It is often used in calendering and injection molding applications. Both types of processing aids improve the melt flow properties of PVC, resulting in smoother and more efficient processing of PVC film.</p></p>
