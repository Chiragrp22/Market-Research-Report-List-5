Auger screens use a rotating screw to remove debris from wastewater, while bar screens use stationary or moving bars to capture solid waste. Drum screens consist of a rotating drum that filters out solids. These equipment types are crucial for wastewater treatment plants to effectively remove large particles and debris from the water, ensuring proper filtration and treatment processes.</p></p>
