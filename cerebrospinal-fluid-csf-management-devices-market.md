<p><ul><li>CSF Shunts</li><li>CSF Drainage Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cerebrospinal Fluid (CSF) Management Devices market includes CSF Shunts and CSF Drainage Systems. CSF Shunts are devices used to divert excess CSF from the brain to another part of the body where it can be absorbed. CSF Drainage Systems, on the other hand, are used to drain excess CSF from the brain and allow for monitoring of CSF pressure. Both types of devices are important in the management of conditions such as hydrocephalus and intracranial pressure disorders.</p></p>
