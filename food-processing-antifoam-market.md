<p><p>Food processing antifoam can be classified into three main types based on their composition- water-based, silicone-based, and oil-based. Water-based antifoams are made of non-toxic ingredients and are commonly used in food processing industries. Silicone-based antifoams are widely used due to their effectiveness in controlling foam and their thermal stability. Oil-based antifoams are made from mineral or vegetable oils and are typically used in high-temperature applications. Each type of antifoam has its own unique properties and is selected based on the specific requirements of the food processing process.</p></p>
