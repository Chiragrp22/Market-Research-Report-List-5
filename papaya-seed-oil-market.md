<p><p>Papaya Seed Oil is extracted using two main methods - Chemical Extraction and Physical Squeeze. In Chemical Extraction, the oil is obtained by using solvents such as hexane to separate the oil from the seeds. This method is efficient but may involve the use of harmful chemicals. On the other hand, Physical Squeeze involves crushing the seeds to extract the oil without using any chemicals. This method is considered more natural and organic, making it a preferred choice for health-conscious consumers.</p></p>
