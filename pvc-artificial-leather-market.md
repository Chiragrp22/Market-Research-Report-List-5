<p><p>Calender PVC Leather is a type of PVC artificial leather that is produced by passing PVC resin through a series of heated rollers to give it a smooth and even texture. On the other hand, Rotary Screen Coating PVC Artificial Leather is manufactured by applying PVC resin directly onto a fabric substrate using a rotary screen printing process. Both types of PVC artificial leather are popular choices in the market due to their durability, versatility, and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
