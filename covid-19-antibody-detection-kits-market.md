RDT kits provide quick results by detecting antibodies in a person's blood sample. ELISA kits are sensitive and accurate, measuring the levels of antibodies in a more detailed manner. Neutralization Assay kits determine the ability of antibodies to neutralize the virus. Each type serves a specific purpose in identifying and monitoring COVID-19 infections.</p></p>
