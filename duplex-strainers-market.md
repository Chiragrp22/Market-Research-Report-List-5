<p><p>Duplex Strainers are industrial filtration devices used to remove solid particles from liquid flow systems. They consist of two separate filter chambers, allowing for continuous operation as one chamber can be cleaned or serviced while the other remains in operation. These strainers are widely used in industries such as petrochemical, water treatment, food and beverage, and pharmaceuticals to protect pumps, valves, and other equipment from damage caused by debris.</p><p>The Duplex Strainers Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.6% during the forecast period.
