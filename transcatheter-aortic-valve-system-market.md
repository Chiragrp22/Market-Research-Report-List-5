<p><p>The Transcatheter Aortic Valve System is a minimally invasive procedure used to treat patients with severe aortic stenosis who are deemed high-risk for traditional open-heart surgery. This system involves inserting a replacement valve through a catheter, typically through the femoral artery, and placing it into the diseased valve. This approach reduces the need for open-heart surgery and allows for quicker recovery times.</p><p>The Transcatheter Aortic Valve System Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.1% during the forecast period.
