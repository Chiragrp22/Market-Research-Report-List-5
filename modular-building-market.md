<p><p>Modular building market can be divided into two main types: Stationary Tiny House and Mobile Tiny House. Stationary Tiny Houses are built on a permanent foundation and do not have the ability to be moved once constructed. These types of homes are typically larger and provide more space for living. On the other hand, Mobile Tiny Houses are built on trailers and designed to be easily transportable, allowing owners to move their home to different locations. This market segment caters to individuals looking for flexibility and mobility in their living spaces.</p></p>
