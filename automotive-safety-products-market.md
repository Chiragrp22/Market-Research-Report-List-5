<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automotive Safety Products Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Active automotive safety products are those that help prevent accidents from happening in the first place, such as anti-lock brake systems, traction control, and lane departure warning systems. These products actively intervene to assist the driver in avoiding potential dangers on the road. Passive automotive safety products, on the other hand, are designed to minimize the impact of an accident after it has occurred, such as airbags, seat belts, and crumple zones. These products are activated during a collision to protect the occupants of the vehicle.</p></p>
