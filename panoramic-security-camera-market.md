<p><p>Panoramic security cameras incorporate a wide-angle lens to provide a broader view of a location. There are two main types of panoramic security camera markets: IP cameras and analog cameras. Analog cameras, on the other hand, use traditional wiring for video transmission and are often considered more budget-friendly. Both types are used in various security applications, with IP cameras being preferred for their advanced features and image quality.</p></p>
