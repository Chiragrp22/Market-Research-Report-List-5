<p><p>An electromagnetic starter is an electrical device used to start and stop electric motors in industrial applications. It consists of a contactor, overload relay, and a protection device, all housed in a single enclosure. When the starter is energized, the contactor closes to allow current to flow to the motor, starting it up. The overload relay protects the motor from overheating by detecting abnormal current levels.</p><p>The Electromagnetic Starter Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.10% during the forecast period. <p><p>Electromagnetic starters can be categorized into two main types: single-phase and three-phase electromagnetic starters. Single-phase electromagnetic starters are designed to control electrical equipment or motors that operate on a single-phase power supply, typically used in household appliances or small machinery. On the other hand, three-phase electromagnetic starters are commonly used for more heavy-duty industrial applications, where three-phase power supply is required for larger motors or equipment. Both types play a crucial role in controlling and protecting the electrical system in various applications.</p></p>
