<p><p>Sublimation ink is categorized into two main types: Water-Based Dye Sublimation Ink and Solvent Dye Sublimation Ink. Water-Based Dye Sublimation Ink utilizes water as a solvent, making it environmentally friendly and ideal for use in home printers. On the other hand, Solvent Dye Sublimation Ink uses a different chemical solvent, making it more durable and suitable for industrial printing applications. Both types of ink are used in the sublimation process to create vibrant and long-lasting images on various substrates like textiles, ceramics, and metals.</p></p>
