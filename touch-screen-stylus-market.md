<p><p>Touch screen stylus market can be divided into two main types - active and passive stylus. Active stylus require power source and have additional features like pressure sensitivity and palm rejection, making them more precise and versatile for tasks like drawing and note-taking. Passive stylus, on the other hand, do not require power source and are simpler in design, mainly used for navigation and basic touch screen functions. Both types cater to different user needs and preferences in the touch screen stylus market.</p></p>
