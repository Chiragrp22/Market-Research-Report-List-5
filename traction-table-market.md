<p><p>Traction tables are used in physical therapy to stretch and decompress the spine. There are two main types of traction tables on the market: electric adjustment and manual adjustment. Electric adjustment tables use motorized mechanisms to change the table's position, making it easier for the therapist to control and adjust. Manual adjustment tables, on the other hand, require physical effort from the therapist to change the table's position. Both types of tables have their own benefits and drawbacks, depending on the specific needs of the therapist and patient.</p></p>
