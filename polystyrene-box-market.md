<p><p>Polystyrene boxes are commonly used in packaging and insulation due to their lightweight and insulating properties. The two main types of polystyrene boxes are open cell foam and closed cell foam. Open cell foam is more porous, allowing air and moisture to pass through, making it ideal for cushioning and soundproofing. Closed cell foam, on the other hand, is denser and more rigid, providing better insulation and protection against impact. Both types have their unique applications in various industries, such as food storage, shipping, and construction.</p></p>
