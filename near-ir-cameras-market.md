<p><strong>The Near IR Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CCD NIR Cameras</li><li>CMOS NIR Cameras</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Near IR Cameras are classified into three main types - CCD NIR Cameras, CMOS NIR Cameras, and Others. CCD NIR Cameras utilize Charge-Coupled Device technology to capture near-infrared images with high sensitivity and low noise. CMOS NIR Cameras employ Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor technology for efficient image processing and lower power consumption. The Others category includes specialized NIR cameras with unique features or applications, such as SWIR or InGaAs cameras. Each type offers distinct advantages and is utilized in various industries such as healthcare, agriculture, and security.</p></p>
