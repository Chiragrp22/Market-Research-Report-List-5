<p><p>Knee bracing devices market consists of different types such as prophylactic braces, functional braces, rehabilitative braces, and unloader braces. Prophylactic braces are used to prevent knee injuries during sports or physical activities. Functional braces provide support to an injured knee during movement. Rehabilitative braces are designed to aid in the recovery process after a knee injury or surgery. Unloader braces are used to alleviate pain and pressure on the knee joints, particularly for individuals with osteoarthritis.</p></p>
