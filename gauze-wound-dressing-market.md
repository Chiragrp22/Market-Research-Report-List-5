<p><p>Gauze wound dressings come in two main types: woven gauze and nonwoven gauze. Woven gauze is made by weaving together fibers to create a strong and durable dressing. Nonwoven gauze, on the other hand, is made by bonding fibers together without weaving, resulting in a soft and smooth texture. Both types of gauze dressings are commonly used in healthcare settings for wound care due to their absorbent and breathable properties, providing a protective barrier for wounds.</p></p>
