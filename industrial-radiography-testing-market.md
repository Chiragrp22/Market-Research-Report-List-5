<p><p>Industrial radiography testing market involves various types of methods for inspecting materials and components for defects. Film radiography uses traditional X-ray film to capture images, while computed radiography digitizes the images for easier storage and analysis. Direct radiography utilizes digital detectors for real-time imaging. Computed tomography creates 3D images by combining multiple X-ray images taken at different angles. Each method has its own benefits and can be used in different applications within the industrial radiography testing market.</p></p>
