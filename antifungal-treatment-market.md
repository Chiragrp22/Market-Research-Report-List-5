<p><p>Antifungal treatment market can be classified into five main types: Azoles, Echinocandins, Polyenes, Allylamines, and Other. Azoles are the most commonly used antifungal medications, inhibiting the synthesis of ergosterol in fungal cell membranes. Echinocandins work by inhibiting cell wall synthesis in fungi. Polyenes bind to ergosterol and disrupt fungal cell membranes. Allylamines inhibit ergosterol synthesis. The "Other" category includes various antifungal medications with different mechanisms of action. Each type of antifungal medication targets specific aspects of fungal growth and infection.</p></p>
