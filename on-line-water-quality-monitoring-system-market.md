<p><p>On-Line Water Quality Monitoring Systems utilizing the Electrode Method involve sensors that measure various parameters like pH, conductivity, turbidity, etc., providing real-time water quality data. On the other hand, systems utilizing Spectrophotometry technology analyze water samples by passing light through them to determine parameters like chemical oxygen demand, nitrogen levels, etc. Both these markets offer solutions for continuous monitoring of water quality to ensure its safety and purity, with the Electrode Method focusing on in-line monitoring and Spectrophotometry on laboratory analysis.</p></p>
