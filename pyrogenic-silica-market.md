<p><p>Pyrogenic silica, also known as fumed silica, is a high performance additive used in various industries such as rubber, pharmaceuticals, food, personal care, and coatings. Pyrogenic silica is valued for its high surface area, low density, and excellent dispersibility.</p><p>The global Pyrogenic Silica Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.20% during the forecast period.
