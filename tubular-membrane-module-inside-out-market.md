<p><p>Tubular membrane modules for filtration are classified into two main types: inorganic membrane and organic membrane. Inorganic membranes are made from materials like ceramic or metals, offering high resistance to harsh chemicals and temperatures. Organic membranes are constructed from polymers, providing flexibility and high permeability. Both types have their own advantages and are used in various industries like water treatment, food and beverage, and pharmaceuticals. The choice between inorganic and organic membranes depends on the specific filtration requirements of the application.</p></p>
