<p><p>Data Bus Couplers are used in electronic systems to provide galvanic isolation for data communication. There are three main types of data bus couplers: cassette, in-line, and others. Cassette couplers are compact units that can be easily inserted into a system, making them ideal for applications where space is limited. In-line couplers are designed to be installed in a straight line within a system, providing a seamless connection between devices. Other types of couplers may include customized or hybrid configurations to meet specific system requirements.</p></p>
