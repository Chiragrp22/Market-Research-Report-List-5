<p><p>Automatic peeling machines are available in two main types: single channel and dual channel. Single channel machines are designed to peel one piece of produce at a time, making them ideal for smaller operations or those requiring more manual control. Dual channel machines, on the other hand, are able to peel two pieces of produce simultaneously, increasing efficiency and productivity for larger operations. Both types of machines offer automated peeling capabilities, but their throughput capacities and level of automation vary to suit different market needs.</p></p>
