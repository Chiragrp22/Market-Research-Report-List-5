<p><p>Fasciotomy devices market includes various types such as Split-Thickness Skin Grafts (STSG), Negative Pressure Wound Therapy (NPWT), Vessel Loop Techniques, Continuous External Tissue Expansion (CETE), and other devices. STSG involves transferring a thin layer of skin from a donor site to cover a wound. NPWT utilizes negative pressure to promote healing in wounds. Vessel Loop Techniques help in controlling bleeding during surgeries. CETE is used for tissue expansion before skin grafting. Other devices in the market cater to various specific needs in fasciotomy procedures.</p></p>
