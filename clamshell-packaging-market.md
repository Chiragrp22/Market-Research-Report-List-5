<p><p>Clamshell packaging is available in various types such as PE (polyethylene), PP (polypropylene), PET (polyethylene terephthalate), PVC (polyvinyl chloride), and others. PE clamshell packaging is known for its flexibility and durability. PP clamshells are lightweight and offer good protection. PET clamshells are transparent and have good barrier properties. PVC clamshells are cost-effective and provide good visibility. Other types of clamshell packaging may include materials like PLA (polylactic acid) or PS (polystyrene), each offering unique benefits for different packaging needs.</p></p>
