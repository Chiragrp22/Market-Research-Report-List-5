<p><p>Fluorescent light bulb disposal market types include distillation reuse and crushing disposal. Distillation reuse involves separating the valuable components of used bulbs for recycling or repurposing. This method minimizes waste and reduces environmental impact. Crushing disposal, on the other hand, involves breaking down bulbs into smaller pieces for safe disposal. This method helps prevent mercury and other harmful substances from being released into the environment. Both distillation reuse and crushing disposal play crucial roles in managing the environmental impact of fluorescent light bulb waste.</p></p>
