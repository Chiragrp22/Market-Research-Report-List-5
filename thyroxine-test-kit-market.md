<p><p>The Thyroxine Test Kit market includes various types of testing methods such as Chemiluminescence Immunoassay and Time-resolved Immunoassay. Chemiluminescence Immunoassay is a technique that uses light emission to detect the presence of thyroxine in a sample, while Time-resolved Immunoassay uses a time-resolved fluorescence emission to measure the levels of thyroxine. Both methods are commonly used for diagnosing thyroid disorders and monitoring thyroid hormone levels in patients. They provide accurate and precise results in a timely manner.</p></p>
