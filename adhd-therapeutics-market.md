<p><p>ADHD therapeutics market primarily consists of two types of medications: stimulants and non-stimulants. Stimulants work by increasing dopamine and norepinephrine levels in the brain, helping improve focus and attention. Non-stimulants, on the other hand, work differently by targeting other neurotransmitters such as norepinephrine or alpha-2 adrenergic receptors. Both types of medications are commonly prescribed to treat ADHD symptoms, with the choice between them depending on individual patient needs and preferences.</p></p>
