<p><p>The IC substrate material market consists of various types of materials, including substrate resin, copper foil, insulation materials, drill, and other miscellaneous materials. Substrate resin is used as the base material for the substrate, providing structural support. Copper foil is used for the circuitry within the substrate. Insulation materials are utilized to prevent electrical interference. Drills are used for creating holes in the substrate for components. Other materials may include adhesives, solder masks, and protective coatings for additional functionality and protection.</p></p>
