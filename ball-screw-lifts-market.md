<p><p>Ball screw lifts are categorized into different types based on their functionality. Rotary ball screw lifts are designed to provide rotational motion, making them suitable for applications requiring precise positioning and control. Translation ball screw lifts, on the other hand, provide linear motion and are commonly used in lifting heavy loads or moving items along a straight path. Other types of ball screw lifts include customized designs for specific applications that do not fall under the rotary or translation categories.</p></p>
