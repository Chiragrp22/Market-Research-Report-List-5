<p><p>Compressor dehumidifiers come in different types in the market, including tower type and rotor type. Tower type dehumidifiers are typically taller and slimmer in design, making them suitable for smaller spaces. They work by pulling in air through a filter and cooling it to remove moisture. Rotor type dehumidifiers, on the other hand, use a rotating wheel to absorb moisture from the air. They are more energy-efficient and effective in large spaces, making them a popular choice for industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
