<p><p>Continuous feeding type neonatal infant enteral feeding pumps are designed to deliver a steady flow of formula or breast milk over an extended period of time, usually 24 hours. This type of pump is ideal for infants who require a constant supply of nutrients. On the other hand, cycled feeding type pumps deliver formula or breast milk in predetermined intervals, allowing for periods of rest between feedings. This type of pump is commonly used for infants who can tolerate larger volumes of feedings at once.</p></p>
