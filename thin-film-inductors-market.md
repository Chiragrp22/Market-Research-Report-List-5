<p><p>Thin film inductors are categorized into two main types in the market: SMD Type and Plug-in Type. SMD Type inductors are surface mount devices that are smaller in size and more suitable for compact electronic equipment. On the other hand, Plug-in Type inductors are larger in size and require manual insertion into circuit boards. Both types offer different advantages, with SMD Type being more space-efficient and Plug-in Type providing easier assembly and servicing capabilities.</p></p>
