Robotic Total Stations are equipped with motorized drives and can be operated remotely, increasing efficiency and accuracy on job sites. Manual Total Stations require physical adjustment by the operator and are typically used for smaller-scale projects. Both types offer high levels of accuracy and precision in measuring distances and angles, catering to the diverse needs of the surveying and construction industry.</p></p>
