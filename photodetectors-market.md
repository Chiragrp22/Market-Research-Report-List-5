<p><p>Photodetectors are devices that convert light signals into electrical signals. There are three main types of photodetectors in the market. Junction photodetectors utilize a pn junction to generate an electrical current in response to light. Avalanche photodetectors use an internal multiplication process to amplify the signal. Other types of photodetectors include photodiodes, phototransistors, and photoconductors, each with their own unique characteristics and applications. These different types cater to a wide range of industries and technologies requiring light detection and measurement.</p></p>
