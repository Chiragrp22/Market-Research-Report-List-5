<p><p>Meniscal repair market can be divided into two main types - All-inside repair and Outside-in repair. All-inside repair involves using devices that are placed entirely within the joint, alongside the meniscus, allowing for a minimally invasive approach. Outside-in repair involves using instruments inserted from the outside of the joint to repair the meniscus. Both techniques have their own advantages and disadvantages, and the choice between the two depends on the specific needs and preferences of the patient and surgeon.</p></p>
