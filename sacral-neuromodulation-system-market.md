<p><p>Sacral Neuromodulation System market includes two main types: Internal Neuromodulation System and External Neuromodulation System. Internal Neuromodulation System involves implanting a device, usually a neurostimulator, directly into the body to deliver electrical impulses to the sacral nerves for treating conditions like overactive bladder and fecal incontinence. On the other hand, External Neuromodulation System involves using a device outside the body to deliver electrical stimulation to the sacral nerves through the skin, offering a less invasive option for managing similar conditions.</p></p>
